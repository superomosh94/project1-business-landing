The story behind Derivo Tech Solutions - Driving tech for the future.

Our Story

At Derivo Tech Solutions, we don’t just build websites. We build digital engines for growth. We believe the future of Kenyan business is online, and our story is about empowering that future—one line of code, one partnership at a time.

The Spark - Why We Started

The idea for Derivo wasn’t born in a boardroom, but in the everyday hustle of Nairobi’s entrepreneurs. We kept hearing the same story: brilliant business owners with incredible potential, held back by a digital world that felt too slow, too complex, or too expensive. Agencies offered promises that took months to deliver, while DIY tools created generic sites that couldn't scale. There was a gap between vision and reality.

We asked a simple question: What if getting a powerful, custom-coded website wasn't a marathon, but a sprint? What if a business could go from concept to a live, high-performance site in days, not months, and start growing immediately? That question became our mission.

Our Driving Philosophy

Our name, Derivo (from "to derive"), reflects our core belief: that powerful solutions are derived from a deep understanding of the problem.
