Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy.

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Derivo Tech Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage our services. We comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019) to ensure your information is handled with care.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you expressed an interest in obtaining information about us or our services, such as:

  • Name and Contact Data (Email address, phone number)
  • Business Information (Company name, website requirements)
  • Communications (Any messages you send us via contact forms or WhatsApp)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service.
  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Communicate with you regarding project updates and quotes.
  • Fulfill any other purpose for which you provided the information.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We only share information when necessary to provide our services or when required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

5. Your Rights

Under the Data Protection Act, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at derivotechsolutions@gmail.com.
